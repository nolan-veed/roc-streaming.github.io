Roc Droid¶

Roc Droid is a proof-of-concept app for Android that can:

  • receive audio from network and play to local audio device

  • capture audio from apps or microphone and send to network

The app is fully interoperable with other Roc receivers and senders based on libroc, including Roc command-line tools and sound server modules. The app is based on Roc JNI bindings.
